ABOUT US

BB Bank has a strong background in banking services for almost 70 years since the creation of the first tennis courts in Coimbatore,
as a matter of fact in South India! Perks was the brainchild of the Late Rama Ranganathan,
a visionary and Lok Shree Awardee, set up the Coimbatore District Tennis Association (CDTA) and promoted the sports by hosting
the first ever Tennis tournament in 1958. He then became the driving force to bring the world-famous Davis Cup to
Coimbatore in 1975. He was solely responsible for bringing Tennis as a sport and many world class players to this small town in
India almost 4 decades ago. The most famous events that was one of a kind in its time was the 48 hour and 72-hour
Nonstop Tennis, which was very inspiring for people to pursue the sport professionally. This boom in Tennis gave birth to other sports in Perks.
The First ever swimming pool in South India was built in Perks in the year 1987 with world class facilities and had produced multiple state
and district swimmers for many years. Sports grew in Perks with the formation of the Basketball, Volleyball & Football Teams and
individual achievements in Tennis, Swimming, Badminton, Table Tennis & Karate. Perks has always given importance
to sport and has supported many state and National competitors by giving them extra time off school to train
and by giving extra classes for sports professionals. This support gave way to glorious achievements in multiple sport!



VISION

The Bank's vision is to create a community where people are engaged in various sporting activities creating a competitive environment
where they can test their skills and talents whilst having fun. The future vision is taken into two different phases,
the first is to make Coimbatore a land mark for multiple International sporting events and second is to
train young children in aspirations to compete in National and International levels.



MISSION

Our mission is to create a highly integrated banking facility that provides various services to all age groups regardless of their ability or skill.
We set out to create a system where sport is perceived in a more holistic manner and shall help us to understand the
importance of sporting knowledge. Our mission is set out as follows




  • Provide high quality of service with trained professionals
  • Provide an ambient environment necessary for the best sporting activity
  • Ensure proper maintenance of services, equipment and facility
  • Create Youth & Pro academies and provide a competitive environment for children
  • Host routine events that promote every individual sport and provide opportunities for competitive experiences
  • Engage in regular leagues for every sport and create a better competitive platform in Coimbatore
  • Engage the teams in International events and mark Coimbatore as a base for high performing individuals in different sport.
